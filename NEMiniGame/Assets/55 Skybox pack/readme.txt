================55 SKYBOX pack===============

Requires Unity 4.3.4 or higher.

This pack includes 55 morning-day-evening skyboxes with different color and style variations (realistic-fantasy).
Each of the skyboxes contains 6 textures (2048 x 2048 in PNG format per each texture) and standard Unity skybox material.
All textures are imported at 512 x 512 for fast and correct import process in your Unity. You can adjust resolution up to 2048 in inspector for higher quality. 

Usage: 
Just put one of 55 material (from [Sky_materials] folder) into Edit > Render settings > Skybox material.
